Notes

If something blocks you

Daily 10am standup is the place to flag. If something is critical and you cannot wait, ping Alex directly. Don't sit on a blocker — the launch runway is tight.

Brand discipline reminders

Magenta = #B8255A · cream = #FAF8F6 · black = #0A0A0A. Times New Roman for headings (italic for brand mark). Helvetica Neue for body. No em-dashes in body copy. DSHEA disclaimer adjacent to any structure-function claim. "Anti-aging," "clinically proven," and "FDA approved" never appear anywhere.

Why the master files matter

Every system doc you build this week is a force multiplier for everything we ship after. A locked Klaviyo template means the next 8 emails take an hour each, not a day. A locked carousel template means the next 88 carousels (per the 100-Ads framework) ship at velocity. Build once. Use forever.
