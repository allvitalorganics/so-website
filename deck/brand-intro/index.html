The problem
03 / 15

Half the story.

Most supplements use the right words. Few publish the actual amounts.

Proprietary blends list ingredients without per-ingredient doses. The clinically studied dose appears in the published paper. A different number, often unstated, sits in the bottle. The label says "blend" because the per-ingredient amount would not survive being printed.

The discipline
05 / 15

Only the problems supplements can truly help.

Story is where we thrive. Mechanism is where we earn the right to tell it.

Many supplement categories are built on marketing more than on mechanism. We are not interested in those. We pick categories where peer-reviewed evidence exists at a dose that fits in one daily unit. The rest, we leave alone.

5g and 2.5g arms both produced statistically significant skin elasticity improvement at 4 and 8 weeks (Proksch 2014 elasticity RCT). The Verisol®-specific wrinkle RCT, conducted at 2.5g/d, demonstrated 20% reduction in eye wrinkle volume after 8 weeks plus increased dermal procollagen type I and elastin synthesis. Skin Support uses the upper-range 5g dose with full disclosure.
Proksch et al. 2014 (two RCTs)Skin Pharmacol Physiol. n=69 elasticity (2.5g + 5g arms, 8wk DBPC), n=114 wrinkle (2.5g, 8wk DBPC). PMID 23949208 · PMID 24401291
