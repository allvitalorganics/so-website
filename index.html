The science

We don't ask you to trust us.

Our formulation standard

Four rules for every product we make.

These apply to Skin Support, Bloat Support, Sleep Support, Stress Support, Energy Support — and any future SKU. The product changes. The discipline does not.

01

Every active. Every dose. On the label.

No proprietary blends. No "complex" hiding individual amounts. The Supplement Facts panel discloses every active and the exact amount per serving. If we can't disclose it, we don't add it.

02

Doses match published clinical studies.

When peer-reviewed RCTs exist for an ingredient, we formulate at or near the studied dose — never the trace amount. Where the literature shows a range, we choose toward the upper studied dose, within established safety limits.

03

Sources, forms, and bioavailability disclosed.

We state the supplier where it matters (Verisol® by GELITA AG · Germany) and the chemical form where it changes absorption (Sodium Hyaluronate, D-Alpha Tocopherol, Calcium D-Pantothenate). Form is part of dose.

04

Manufactured under cGMP. Tested before sale.

21 CFR Part 111 compliance. Identity, potency, heavy metals, and microbial testing on every production lot. Korean Certificate of Origin for KORUS FTA. FSVP-documented import path.

Verisol® Collagen Peptides — 5,000mg

The most clinically studied collagen peptide for skin. Produced by GELITA AG (Eberbach, Germany). Not generic collagen — a specific bioactive composition optimized for skin outcomes.

Skin Support · ingredient 2 of 7

Hyaluronic Acid (as Sodium Hyaluronate) — 120mg

Oral hyaluronic acid reaches the dermis from inside — deeper than topical creams can penetrate. We use sodium hyaluronate, the sodium salt form, for better stability and oral absorption.

StudyDesignResultSource
Skin moistureRCT, n=60, 12 weeks, 120mg/daySignificant improvement in skin wrinkle depth and moisture vs placebo at 120mg oral HA daily.PubMed 28686544 · Oe 2017
Skin hydrationRCT, n=96, 8 weeks, 120-240mg/dayOral HA significantly increased skin moisture and improved skin condition in subjects with dry skin.PubMed 28020067 · Kawada 2014

Why 120mg: Matches the dose used in the Oe 2017 RCT that showed significant skin moisture improvement. Sodium hyaluronate form is more stable and bioavailable orally than pure hyaluronic acid.

Skin Support · ingredient 3 of 7

Vitamin C (as Ascorbic Acid) — 200mg (222% DV)

Vitamin C is the essential cofactor for collagen synthesis. Without adequate Vitamin C, the body cannot hydroxylate proline and lysine — the amino acids that form collagen's triple-helix structure. Taking collagen without Vitamin C is like having bricks without mortar.

EvidenceDetailSource
Cofactor for collagen synthesisVitamin C is required for the enzymatic hydroxylation of prolyl and lysyl residues in procollagen. Without it, collagen cannot form properly.PubMed 29099763 · Pullar 2017
Skin aging reviewHigher Vitamin C intake associated with better skin appearance, less wrinkling, less dryness. Both dietary and supplemental.Nutrients 2017 · PubMed 29099763
Antioxidant protectionPotent water-soluble antioxidant that protects skin from oxidative stress caused by UV and environmental pollutants.NIH ODS

Why 200mg: RDA is 75-90mg. We provide 222% DV — enough to fully support collagen synthesis and antioxidant needs. Upper limit is 2,000mg. Significant safety headroom.

Skin Support · ingredient 4 of 7

Vitamin E (as D-Alpha Tocopherol) — 30mg (200% DV)

Vitamin E is the body's primary lipid-soluble antioxidant — it protects cell membranes (which are made of lipids) from oxidative damage. It works synergistically with Vitamin C: E neutralizes free radicals in cell membranes, then C regenerates E so it can work again.

EvidenceDetailSource
PhotoprotectionOral Vitamin E supplementation reduces UV-induced skin damage and erythema when combined with Vitamin C. Synergistic effect documented.PubMed 9763287 · Fuchs & Kern 1998
Skin barrierAlpha-tocopherol accumulates in skin tissue and protects against lipid peroxidation — the process that breaks down the skin barrier.NIH ODS

Why D-alpha tocopherol: The natural form (d-alpha) is approximately 2× more bioavailable than the synthetic form (dl-alpha). 30mg = 200% DV. UL is 1,000mg.

Skin Support · ingredient 5 of 7

Beta-Carotene — 6mg

Beta-carotene is a provitamin A carotenoid — a naturally occurring pigment that your body converts to Vitamin A only as needed. It provides antioxidant protection and internal photoprotection.

EvidenceDetailSource
PhotoprotectionOral beta-carotene supplementation provides protection against sunburn (UV-induced erythema). Carotenoids accumulate in skin and act as internal photoprotectors.PubMed 11340098 · Stahl & Sies 2000
Skin health reviewCarotenoids accumulate in skin and protect against photodamage through antioxidant mechanisms.PubMed 22264449 · Stahl & Sies 2012

Why 6mg: The ATBC study that raised concerns used 20mg/day in male smokers — over 3× our dose, in a different population. 6mg is within normal dietary intake (a large carrot contains ~6-8mg). No UL established for food-form beta-carotene per NIH.

Skin Support · ingredient 6 of 7

Riboflavin / Vitamin B2 — 2mg (154% DV)

Riboflavin is essential for cellular energy production and plays a direct role in skin cell metabolism. It supports the function of flavoenzymes involved in collagen crosslinking and maintenance.

EvidenceDetailSource
Skin metabolismRiboflavin deficiency causes skin disorders. Adequate B2 is required for normal skin cell turnover and repair.NIH ODS
Collagen crosslinkingFMN and FAD (riboflavin-dependent coenzymes) are involved in oxidative reactions necessary for collagen crosslinking — the process that gives collagen its structural strength.PubMed 32935238 · Suwannasom 2020

Why 2mg: RDA 1.1-1.3mg. We provide 154% DV. No UL (water-soluble; excess excreted).

Skin Support · ingredient 7 of 7

Pantothenic Acid / Vitamin B5 — 5mg (100% DV)

Pantothenic acid is the precursor to coenzyme A (CoA) — one of the most important molecules in cellular metabolism. CoA is essential for fatty acid synthesis and metabolism, directly supporting the skin's lipid barrier.

EvidenceDetailSource
Skin barrierPantothenic acid supports epidermal barrier function through its role in lipid synthesis. Topical and oral dexpanthenol have documented wound healing and barrier repair effects.PubMed 12113650 · Ebner 2002
Wound healingSupplemental pantothenic acid has been shown to accelerate wound closure and increase fibroblast proliferation.NIH ODS

Why calcium d-pantothenate: Most stable and bioavailable salt form. 5mg = 100% DV (Adequate Intake). No UL established.

"Fairy dusting" means adding just enough of an ingredient to put it on the label, not enough for it to do anything. Verisol® RCTs used 2,500mg and 5,000mg. We use 5,000mg.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Product

What is Skin Support?
A daily dietary supplement in dual-cap format — 25ml pineapple-flavored liquid shot sealed beneath a cap holding 2 tablets. 7 active ingredients including 5,000mg Verisol® collagen peptides, 120mg hyaluronic acid, 200mg Vitamin C, and 4 supporting vitamins. One unit per day.
How do I take it?
Twist open the cap. Take the 2 tablets. Drink the 25ml pineapple-flavored shot. Done in under 30 seconds. No water, scoop, or blender. One unit daily, preferably morning.
What makes Skin Support different from other collagen supplements?
Three things. (1) We use Verisol® at 5,000mg — the highest clinically studied dose. Verisol is a specific bioactive peptide, not generic collagen. (2) Every ingredient and dose is on the label — no proprietary blends. (3) The dual-cap format combines liquid collagen for rapid absorption with stable vitamin tablets in one sealed unit.
What is Verisol®?
A specific brand of bioactive collagen peptides produced by GELITA AG (Germany). The most clinically studied collagen peptide for skin outcomes — published RCTs measure improvements in elasticity, wrinkle depth, and nail health. Read the research →
What does "no proprietary blend" mean?
A "proprietary blend" is a legal labeling option that lets brands list multiple ingredients without disclosing individual amounts (only the total). You don't know how much of each you're getting. We do not use proprietary blends. Every ingredient and dose is on the Supplement Facts panel.
How long until I see results?
Collagen supplementation is not overnight. Verisol® RCTs measured improvements at 4-8 weeks of daily use. We recommend taking Skin Support daily for at least 8-12 weeks before evaluating. Results vary by individual.*

Safety + Allergens

Is Skin Support safe?
Manufactured under GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) standards. All ingredients are at doses within established safe intake levels per the NIH Office of Dietary Supplements. As with any supplement, consult your healthcare provider before starting — especially if you are pregnant, nursing, or taking medication.
Does Skin Support contain allergens?
The collagen in Skin Support may be derived from fish. If you have a fish allergy, please check the allergen statement on the label or contact us before purchasing. Skin Support is free from gluten, dairy, soy, and artificial colors.
Is sensitive ones FDA approved?
The FDA does not approve dietary supplements. All US dietary supplements must comply with FDA regulations including cGMP (21 CFR Part 111), proper labeling (21 CFR 101.36), and truthful advertising. We comply with all applicable FDA regulations.
Where is Skin Support manufactured?
In Korea at an FDA-registered, HACCP-approved, GMP-certified facility, shipped to the US under KORUS FTA Certificate of Origin. Every production lot is tested for identity, potency, heavy metals, and microbial contamination before sale.
