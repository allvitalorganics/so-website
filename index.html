Frequently asked questions.

What is Skin Support?
Skin Support is a daily dietary supplement in a dual-cap format — a 25ml pineapple-flavored liquid shot sealed beneath a cap that holds 2 tablets. It contains 8 active ingredients including 5,000mg Verisol® collagen peptides, 120mg hyaluronic acid, 200mg Vitamin C, and 5 supporting vitamins and botanical extract. One unit per day.
How do I take it?
Twist open the cap. Take the 2 tablets. Drink the 25ml pineapple-flavored liquid shot. Done — under 30 seconds. No water, no scoop, no blender needed. Take one unit daily, preferably in the morning.
What makes Skin Support different from other collagen supplements?
Three things: (1) We use Verisol® at 5,000mg — that's 2× the dose used in the published clinical studies, and it's a specific bioactive peptide, not generic collagen. (2) We publish every ingredient and every dose on the label — no proprietary blends. (3) The dual-cap format combines liquid collagen (for rapid absorption) with vitamin tablets (for stability) in one sealed unit.
What is Verisol®?
Verisol® is a specific brand of bioactive collagen peptides produced by GELITA AG in Germany. It is the most clinically studied collagen peptide for skin outcomes, with published randomized controlled trials showing improvements in skin elasticity, wrinkle depth, and nail health. It is not generic "collagen peptides" — it is a specific optimized composition. Read the research →
What does "no proprietary blend" mean?
A "proprietary blend" is a legal loophole that allows supplement brands to list multiple ingredients without disclosing individual amounts — only the total weight of the blend. This means you have no idea how much of each ingredient you're actually getting. Sensitive Ones does not use proprietary blends. Every ingredient and its exact dose is published on our Supplement Facts panel.
How long until I see results?
Collagen supplementation is not an overnight change. The published clinical studies on Verisol® measured improvements at 4-8 weeks of daily use at 2.5g. We recommend taking Skin Support daily for at least 8-12 weeks before evaluating results. Individual experiences will vary.*
Is Skin Support safe?
Skin Support is manufactured under GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) standards. All ingredients are used at doses within established safe intake levels as defined by the NIH Office of Dietary Supplements. As with any dietary supplement, we recommend consulting your healthcare provider before starting, especially if you are pregnant, nursing, or taking medication.
Does Skin Support contain allergens?
The collagen in Skin Support may be derived from fish. If you have a fish allergy, please check the allergen statement on the product label or contact us before purchasing. Skin Support is free from gluten, dairy, soy, and artificial colors.
How does the subscription work?
When you subscribe, you receive a fresh box of 28 units every month at $69/month (30% off the one-time price of $99). Shipping is free on all subscriptions. You can skip a month, pause, or cancel anytime from your account — no contracts, no penalties, no questions asked.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with Skin Support, contact us within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Read our full refund policy →
Where is Skin Support manufactured?
Skin Support is manufactured in Korea by a GMP-certified facility and shipped to the US. The product is formulated to meet all US FDA requirements for dietary supplements and is tested for identity, potency, and purity before sale.
Is Sensitive Ones FDA approved?
The FDA does not approve dietary supplements. However, all dietary supplements sold in the US must comply with FDA regulations including Good Manufacturing Practice (21 CFR Part 111), proper labeling (21 CFR 101.36), and truthful advertising. Sensitive Ones complies with all applicable FDA regulations.
